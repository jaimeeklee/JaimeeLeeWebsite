Slo Bitters Branding

This was a university based assignment in which we were to choose a start up company and create a text based poster and logos for. We were to focus on appropriate use of colour, shape and typography to communicate the brand’s core message and overall aesthetic.

I chose to design for ‘Slo Bitters’, which is a Handcrafted bitters company that uses only the best seasonal and locally sourced fruits and botanics. I used muted earthy colours paired with organic and professional looking typefaces to create a balance between the handcrafted and sophisticated feel of the brand. Solutions were created on Adobe Illustrator.
