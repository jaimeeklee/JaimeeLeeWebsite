About Me

Jaimee Lee is a multidisciplinary creative based in Melbourne with a degree in Digital and Social Media and a background in Graphic Design. With a curious mind and a thirst for learning, she is in pursuit of a career that will focus on creating a more positive and sustainable future through the creation of meaningful content and storytelling.

She enjoys working in a broad range of areas, however her strengths lie in graphic design, photography, branding, web design and social media. In her spare time, she enjoys cooking, bushwalking, surfing and spinning vinyl.
