Otways Exploration

A series of images taken on a DSLR camera when travelling down the Great Ocean Road. This personal project aimed to highlight the variety of environment and experiences one could enjoy in this region. It explores and juxtaposes the colours, textures and feelings of wide open spaces like fields and oceans compared to more closed spaces like forests and bushland.
